## Instructions
1. Left Cross Button:
    1. Right Arrow: next song.
    1. Left Arrow: previous song.
    1. Up Arrow: fast foward song.
    1. Down Arrow: delay song.
1. Right 1st Button: play/pause song.
1. Right 2nd Button: mute/unmute song.
